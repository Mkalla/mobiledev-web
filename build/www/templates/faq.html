Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if there are no garages within 10 miles?
If you have Roadside Cover only and we are unable to repair the vehicle within a reasonable time on the roadside, or if repairs are unwise, we will give you a tow of up to 10 miles either to a local garage or an address of your choice. A longer tow may require a surcharge. If you choose our Recovery option in addition to basic Roadside Cover, we shall transport your vehicle to your home or a single address anywhere in the United Kingdom, Jersey, Guernsey and the Isle of Man.
Do you need my identification when in need of recovery?
You must provide your valid membership card and proof of your identity or two other forms of identity to use our service. If these are not available, we may refuse service or make a charge.
Are lost car keys covered?
Yes, we would attempt to unlock your car. However, if we are unable to open your vehicle for any reason, we will arrange for a locksmith to attend where available, but you will be responsible for the costs. If a locksmith is not available, we shall arrange for your vehicle to be taken to a nearby garage for assistance but you will have to pay for any work carried out on the vehicle. Yes - our experienced and friendly UK Rescue call centre teams are on hand 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.
